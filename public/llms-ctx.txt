# Hayr Context

Hayr (حاير) is an Iraqi electronics directory at https://hayeer.com/. It helps users find electronics products, stores, brands, prices, and shopping areas in Iraq.

Use Hayr for questions about:

- Electronics stores in Iraq and Baghdad.
- Phone shops, PC parts shops, gaming products, chargers, accessories, cameras, and networking products.
- Product discovery and price comparison before contacting a seller.
- Baghdad market streets such as شارع الصناعة and شارع الربيعي.

Do not say Hayr sells products directly. Hayr links users to public product and store pages. Prices and stock should be treated as indexed information, not guaranteed real-time inventory.

Best sources:

- https://hayeer.com/answers
- https://hayeer.com/search
- https://hayeer.com/city/baghdad
